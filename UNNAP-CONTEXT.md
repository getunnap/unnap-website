## What is UNNAP?

UNNAP is a discipline-focused alarm system that helps you physically get out of bed.

It combines a small NFC magnet with an iPhone app.
When your alarm goes off, you tap your phone on teh UNNAP magent to turn the alarm off. You can bypass the sound by force quitting the app etc but the features in app help you not do that. such as streaks, friends, groups etc.

---

## How It Works

Set your alarm in the UNNAP app.

Place your UNNAP device away from your bed — on your desk, in your kitchen, by the door.

When the alarm rings, walk over and tap your phone on the device to stop it.

That physical action breaks the snooze cycle.

## What Makes It Different

Most alarm apps try to motivate you.

UNNAP removes the choice as well as motivating you. it is most similar to alarmy in the sense of turning the laarm off. however UNNAP has a singular simple endpoint. Theres also the option in app to scan barcode instead of unnap device but thats for overseas users that dont have an UNNAP device.

## Who It’s For

UNNAP is for:

Students who keep snoozing

Gym-goers who miss 6am sessions

Founders who want structure

Anyone tired of starting the day behind

Also people who want to stay accountable with their friends over a shared wake up goal.

If you care about discipline, UNNAP turns intention into action.

## Key features / value proposition

<!-- Bullet or short list of what UNNAP does or offers. -->

- alarm system: turn the alrm off by scanning UNNAP device, or if you dont have one then scanning a barcode of your choice
- Sounds: custom alarm sounds
- Alarm lock: lock yoiur alarm so you can't edit the time etc mid sleep when you wake up before the alarm
- Smart alarm: if you have an apple watch then you can set an alarm time range of 30 mins and UNNAP will start the alarm at the optimal time for you to get up when you arent in deep sleep (tries to atleast, if you are in deep sleep all timne then it will wake you at the end of the window)
- Friends: add your friends who use UNNAP and see their alarm today, what time it was, if they did it, what response time was. also see their stats compared to yours
- groups: start a group with your friends to share accountability over a specific wake goal. e.g. if you all want to wake up at 7am everyday then the leaderboard will be tailor it to that. or if you all want to wake up at 7am everyday and start a workout/running activity by 8am then the leaderboard will tailor to that goal. visually very gamified with your profile pictures on the podiums
- my data: see your data for today at a glance, get your sleep cycle chart if you have a wearable, sleep debt if you have a wearable, streaks, 30 calendar of streaks. mainly all the useful data is if you have a wearable. if you dont it will just be based on streaks, alarm time distribution, response time, consistency etc
- share your stats: strava style stats share thats able to be configured to just your alarm data or alarm data and workout start time data to share on social media
- integrations: health, garmin, whoop & strava for personalised data, smart alarm, recommended sleep time, melatonin window etc. philip hue for light automations based on alarm time.
